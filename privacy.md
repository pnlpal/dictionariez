# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection and Storage

### Free Version
By default, Dictionariez does not collect any browser information or personal user data. Your lookup history and settings are stored locally in your browser's storage and synced using the browser's built-in sync service.

### Pro Version
If you subscribe to PNL Pro, we collect and store the following data to provide cloud synchronization services:

- **Lookup History**: Words you've looked up and their associated data
- **User Preferences**: Some of your settings and dictionary configurations
- **Account Information**: Basic account details necessary for authentication and service provision

This data is stored securely on our cloud servers to enable synchronization across your devices.

## Cookies and Local Storage

### Free Users
We do not use cookies to store personal information for free users.

### Pro Users
We use cookies and local storage to:
- Maintain your login session
- Store authentication tokens
- Remember your Pro subscription status
- Provide a seamless cross-device experience

## Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with various dictionary websites and services. These third-party services may use cookies or web storage according to their own privacy policies. Dictionariez does not modify or control third-party behavior. Your interactions with these services are governed by their respective privacy policies, just as if you visited their websites directly.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data during transmission and storage. Your Pro account data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

## Your Rights

- **Free Users**: You can clear your local data at any time through your browser settings
- **Pro Users**: You can request deletion of your cloud data by contacting us
- **All Users**: You can opt out of our services at any time
